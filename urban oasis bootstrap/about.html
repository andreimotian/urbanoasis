What we do

We aim for a future-proof and multifunctional Urban Sports Park (USP) to be furnished of at least 1500 m2 with urban sports and recreational facilities who knows how to activate tens to several hundred Maastricht residents to move in public space.

An assessment carried out by us in 2020 shows that there are there is a great need in Maastricht for additional facilities. Skateboarders, break dancers, freerunners and other communities from Maastricht are now working together in our planning, so that we really give citizen participation hands and feet!

Due to its location in the Frontenpark, under the bridge overhang of the renovated Noorderbrug route, Urban Oasis offers opportunities for the facilitation of indoor high-quality sports and recreational facilities, such as a new skate park, a break dance area and a 3x3 basketball court that also serves as a street soccer court: multifunctional, clean, whole, safe and wheelchair accessible.

In addition, we see opportunities for a new inspiring play environment at this location for children with nature education section, boulder climbing elements, others urban sports and street gym elements for the practice of trail, freerunning, slackline, calisthenics and boot camp, as well as exercise options for the elderly.

We have clearly opted for the design of a movement-friendly environment. In this way we aim to involve the community in the changes that are taking place in their living environment. The design and programming of Urban Oasis will be realized by discussing engage with the Maastricht communities. This is how we want to guarantee that Urban Oasis is a becomes an important part of the living environment that facilitates and stimulates people and challenges to meet each other and also to move, play and exercise

The Urban Culture Kiosk located at the USP makes additional functions, activities and events and guarantees sustainable use of the available sports and play facilities. The Urban Culture Kiosk is a Multifunctional Accommodation. Residents, social organizations and entrepreneurs know where to find each other here

In addition to sports and culture in public space, it is also a means to achieve citizenship, ownership, quality of life, social cohesion, a healthy lifestyle, better school and work performance, city marketing and (social) participation.

OUR ASSUMPTIONS - the social added value of URBAN OASIS

  • We encourage (particularly young) people to exercise independently, structurally and remove obstacles;
  • We are increasing the sport (and culture) participation of Maastricht residents with an emphasis on participation of specific target groups (mainly Maastricht youngsters);
  • We do this by opting for urban culture & sports, with which we connect to the contemporary needs regarding sports and cultural participation;
  • We are committed to increasing emancipation in a socially vulnerable situation;
  • We promote physical and mental resilience;
  • We promote personal and social development;
  • We contribute to the health and well-being of Maastricht residents;
  • We contribute to active citizenship among young people
  • We promote equality of opportunity and offer support to those who need it. Every child deserves the opportunity to develop to the fullest;
  • We strengthen ties between communities;
  • We realize the formation of a new Maastricht community: The Urban Oasis community;
  • We contribute to the quality of life in the city;
  • We contribute to the attractiveness of Maastricht for young people;
